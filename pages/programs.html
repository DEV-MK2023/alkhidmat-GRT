Our Programs

Transforming lives through sustainable welfare initiatives, community support, education, healthcare, and humanitarian services.

What We Do

Building Stronger Communities

Alkhidmat Foundation Gujrat works across multiple sectors to uplift vulnerable communities through sustainable welfare programs and humanitarian initiatives.

Orphan Care

Providing shelter, education, healthcare, emotional support, and a nurturing environment for orphaned children.

Health Services

Delivering healthcare support through hospitals, clinics, medical camps, and health awareness initiatives.

Education

Empowering future generations through schools, scholarships, vocational training, and educational support.

Microfinance

Helping families achieve financial independence through small business support and interest-free loans.

Disaster Management

Providing emergency relief, rescue operations, food, shelter, and rehabilitation during disasters.

Clean Water

Ensuring access to safe drinking water through wells, filtration plants, and water supply projects.

Community Services

Supporting communities through food distribution, social welfare, and community development initiatives.

Mawakhat Program

Helping deserving families establish sustainable livelihoods through financial and technical assistance.
