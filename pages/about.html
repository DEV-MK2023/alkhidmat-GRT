Who We Are

A Commitment To Humanity

Alkhidmat Foundation Gujrat is committed to serving humanity through sustainable welfare programs and community development initiatives.

Through healthcare, education, orphan care, disaster management, and social welfare programs, we strive to improve lives and empower communities.

We believe every individual deserves dignity, opportunity, and access to essential services regardless of background or circumstances.

Our Mission

To serve humanity through transparent, impactful, and sustainable welfare programs that create long-term positive change.

Our Vision

A compassionate society where every individual has access to opportunity, support, and a better quality of life.
